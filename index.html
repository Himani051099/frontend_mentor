Simple Omelette Recipie

An omelette is a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Whether you're looking for a quick breakfast, a light lunch, or a satisfying dinner, a well-made omelette never disappoints. This recipe will guide you through the steps to create a perfect, fluffy omelette every time, with tips for customization to suit your taste.

Prepration Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 Minutes
  • Prepration: 5 Minutes
  • Cooking: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

For a basic omelette, you will need:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Optional fillings:

  • Cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, feta, etc.)
  • Vegetables (bell peppers, onions, spinach, mushrooms, tomatoes)
  • Proteins (ham, bacon, sausage, smoked salmon)
  • Herbs (chives, parsley, dill)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl. Add the milk or water, a pinch of salt, and a grind of pepper.
    • Whisk the eggs vigorously until the mixture is well combined and slightly frothy. This will incorporate air into the eggs, making the omelette fluffier.
    • If you're using fillings, chop the vegetables, grate the cheese, and prepare any meats in advance.
  2. Cook the Omelette:
    • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the butter or oil and let it melt and coat the pan evenly.
    • Pour the egg mixture into the pan. Let it cook undisturbed for about 10-15 seconds.
    • Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs, pushing them from the edges towards the center. Tilt the pan to let the uncooked eggs flow to the edges.
    • When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add your desired fillings evenly over one half of the omelette.
  3. Fold and Serve:
    • Carefully fold the omelette in half using the spatula. Let it cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute, depending on how well-done you like your eggs.
    • Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.
